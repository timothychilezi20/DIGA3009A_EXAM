Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit music for review?

Send your music to submissions@thebeatreport.com with a brief bio and links to your work. We review all submissions but cannot guarantee coverage.

Can I advertise on The Beat Report?

Yes! Contact us at advertising@thebeatreport.com for our media kit and advertising rates.

Do you cover events outside Johannesburg?

We cover events nationwide. For event coverage requests, please contact us at least 2 weeks in advance.

How can I write for The Beat Report?

We're always looking for talented writers. Send your writing samples and resume to info@thebeatreport.com.
